About Us

Elevate Your Home’s Appeal with Stage & Style

At Stage and Style, we believe that every space tells a story. Founded by Noor, an expert interior designer with a passion for color and creativity, we focus on bringing fresh and dynamic energy into every project.

Noor has years of experience transforming Airbnb properties, residential homes, and commercial spaces into breathtaking environments that captivate guests and owners alike.

Unlike other staging companies that stick to neutral tones, we specialize in color-infused designs that create memorable experiences. From curated furniture to personalized decor elements, we ensure every space is warm, welcoming, and uniquely yours.

Let’s collaborate to make your vision a reality.

Meet Noor – Founder & CEO of Stage and Style

I’m Noor, the proud founder of Stage and Style, where we blend beauty, functionality, and style to transform spaces that leave a lasting impression.
